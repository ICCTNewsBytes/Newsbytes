About NewsBytes

We are the official campus news publication — student-written, student-run, and dedicated to keeping our university community informed, inspired, and connected.

📰

Our Mission

To deliver accurate, timely, and relevant campus news that reflects the diverse voices of our university community.

🎓

Student-Run

Every story is written, edited, and published by students. NewsBytes is a platform for student journalists to hone their craft.

🏫

Campus-Focused

We cover academics, events, sports, student life, and announcements — all in one place, updated regularly.

🤝

Community First

NewsBytes exists to strengthen our university community by creating a shared space where everyone stays informed.
